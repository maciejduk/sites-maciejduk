I'm proud to announce that I've earned the **Red Hat Certified Specialist in Containers** certification!

## What is this certification?

The Red Hat Certified Specialist in Containers (EX188) validates skills in creating, managing, and deploying containerized applications. This certification demonstrates proficiency in:

- Creating and managing containers with **Podman**
- Building custom container images using **Containerfiles**
- Managing container networking and storage
- Running multi-container applications
- Deploying containers to **Red Hat OpenShift**

## Why containers matter

Containerization has become essential in modern infrastructure. As a Lead SRE working with OpenShift 4, this certification solidifies my expertise in:

- Building reproducible, portable application environments
- Implementing container security best practices
- Orchestrating containerized workloads at scale

This certification builds upon my RHCE foundation and aligns with my day-to-day work managing containerized infrastructure for enterprise clients.
